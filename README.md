# Sales Forecast & Decision Intelligence Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project focuses on sales visibility, regional performance analysis, and forecasting trends using Power BI.

The goal was not just to visualize sales data, but to better understand how operational and revenue patterns evolve over time and how forecasting can support business decisions before problems become expensive.

The dashboard combines sales performance metrics with forecasting analysis to highlight:

* Revenue trends
* Regional sales performance
* Profitability patterns
* Customer activity
* Forecast behavior
* Monthly operational performance

---

## Business Problem

Many dashboards report what already happened.

This project focused on understanding:

* Which regions contribute most to revenue
* How sales and profit change over time
* Whether trends are stable or volatile
* How forecasting can improve operational planning
* Which performance indicators may require attention before revenue declines

The objective was to turn dashboard metrics into business signals rather than static reports.

---

## Key Questions Explored

* Which regions generate the highest revenue?
* How are sales and profit trending month over month?
* Are there noticeable forecasting fluctuations?
* Which periods show unstable sales behavior?
* How can forecasting support planning decisions?
* What operational signals can be identified early?

## Business Insights

Some important analytical observations from the dashboard:

* Revenue performance varied significantly across regions
* Monthly trends highlighted periods of stronger operational performance
* Forecasting visuals revealed possible future volatility patterns
* Profit trends did not always move proportionally with sales
* Regional concentration suggested dependency on specific markets

The project reinforced an important lesson:

Dashboards become valuable when they help businesses understand what may happen next — not only what already happened.

## What I Learned

One of the biggest lessons from this project was that dashboards should support decision-making, not just reporting.

Forecasting becomes far more useful when combined with operational context such as:

* sales movement,
* regional performance,
* customer activity,
* and revenue trends.

This project helped strengthen my understanding of how analytics can support operational and business planning decisions.
